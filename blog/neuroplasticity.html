Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

Understanding neuroplasticity has two main benefits. The first, it encourages learners to engage with work more due to them recognizing that thanks to neuroplasticity growth and improvement in an area is possible. The second is that learners can base their study methods around the principles of neuroplasticity, thus improving the effectiveness of their learning.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit

Reinforce the idea that due to the workings of neuroplasticity it is very possible for me to further my knowledge and understanding and base study methods around how neuroplasticity works, such as focussing group work and consistent daily study.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity

  • Strengths
    • New Skills: Learning new skills such as language or arts.
    • Challenges: By pushing one's self and completeing complex tasks/puzzles.
    • Study Methods: By basing study methods around the workings of neuroplasticity.

    Growth Mindset

    Discuss what it is and why it is relevant

    A growth mindset is the idea that one's intelligence or skills are not set in stone or pre-determined by factors out of their control and can be continuosly improved. It is relevant as by understanding the importance of having growth mindset, we can ensure that studyinging doesn't go to waste by approaching work the wrong way or with a fixed mindset.

    In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

    I hadn't considered that a person could have a different mindset for different areas in life.

    How will you integrate growth mindset into your learning journey?

    Attempt to head into the course with a positive attitude and the intent to grow my skillset and knowledge.
