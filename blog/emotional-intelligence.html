Emotional Intelligence?

What is Emotional Intelligence?

The ability to understand one’s own emotions and feelings.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is a person’s ability to reason and apply logic to problems, whereas EQ is an understanding of a person’s own mind.

Why is EQ important?

Having better EQ means you are able to better understand yourself and thus recognize your faults or when you aren’t being completely unbiased. For example, if I lack EQ I might not immediacy recognize whatever I’m feeling in the moment and thus let my actions and out-look on a situation be influenced by these feelings. On the other hand, if I did have good EQ, I could understand how I’m feeling and ensure I don’t let it influence my decisions unknowingly.
